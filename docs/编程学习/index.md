### 3.dotfiles和配置shell

##### dotfiles

 `bash` - `~/.bashrc`, `~/.bash_profile`
 `git` - `~/.gitconfig`
 `vim` - `~/.vimrc` 和 `~/.vim` 目录
 `ssh` - `~/.ssh/config`
 `tmux` - `~/.tmux.conf`
